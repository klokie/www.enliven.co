Hein van Tonder
Photographer, Stylist
Baking, Cooking

Photographer, Videographer and Food Stylist, Hein van Tonder is a native South African with a combination of local, continental and global creative experiences.

In April 2019 he was awarded Highly Commended in the global Pink Lady Food Photographer of the Year competition having won the Food Bloggers category in 2017. This is the fourth year van Tonder has been a finalist in this world respected competition.

His popular blog and Instagram account Heinstirred feature inspiring easy to make at home recipes and delicious food photography images.

His work includes photography and videography, recipe development, cooking in the kitchen and global tasting of all things food, lifestyle and interiors, always contributing to and featuring his work across popular magazines, blogs, websites and social media accounts for clients across the globe.

His career transition to the creative world has been very recent having packed in a career as an accountant with a calculator for a camera to realise his passion and skill as a Director of Photography.

Represented by Bird On A Wire Artist Management
