Karin Johansson
Photographer, Stylist
Garden, Homes
Karin is the interior designer who decided to become a photographer. After several years at IKEA, she began freelancing and her main focus today is in interior and still life.
"That I can combine my two biggest passions,photographing nbsp;and styling, in my job is my biggest strength".
Her work can be seen in some of the large Swedish interior magazines, such as Sköna Hem, Plaza Interiör and Allt i Hemmet, but also international.
